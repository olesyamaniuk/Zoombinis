About us

Zoombinis  is an engaging puzzle adventure game that challenges players to use logic, strategy, and creativity. Help the adorable Zoombinis navigate tricky paths, solve mind-bending puzzles, and overcome obstacles on their journey to a safe and peaceful home. Perfect for all ages, Zoombinis combines fun gameplay with critical thinking exercises.

FAQ

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, with optional in-app purchases.

  • How do I start the game?

    Download the app, install it, and press 'Play' to begin your adventure.

  • Can I play offline? 

    Yes, Zoombinis supports offline play.

  • What skills does the game develop? 

    The game enhances logic, pattern recognition, and critical thinking.

  • Are there different difficulty levels? 

    Yes, puzzles become progressively more challenging as you play.
